About Me

Aloha! My name is FLo and I am a full-time dad and hubby, part-time gamer. Born and raised in Hawai’i. I joined the US Army in 2009, and had the pleasure of vacationing in the sandstorms of Iraq during Operation New Dawn. I have a beautiful wife named Alyy who I occasionally game with, and a son named Keahe who is a future gaming nerd. I started streaming in 2019 with a MacBookPro and my wife’s PS4. Since then I’ve been upgrading my setup little by little to create better and better content. My schedule is a bit crazy at the moment, but I do keep up with the communities that I’m in. #StrengthThroughSupport

Join Da Ohana
